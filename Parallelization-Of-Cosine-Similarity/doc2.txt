Demonetization is a tool to battle Inflation, Black Money, Corruption and Crime, discourage a cash dependent economy and help trade. Its policy of the government by banning Rs. 500 and Rs. 1000 currency notes has influenced all almost all the corner of the economy. As an example, the large increase in bank deposits during the demonetization period caused a surplus of loanable funds. However, there was almost no impact of this either on the amount of bank loans or in the average lending rate.
